You are loyal and devoting to your friends and rather protective of them. You are not really romantic and often just really good friends with your lover. At times you can be mean, stubborn, and narrow-minded in your view but it is merely your way of being a perfectionist and your need to live by own rules.

You are noted for your diligence and industriousness. You are strong, solid, and practical individual, reputed for your common sense and your ability to keep your feet firmly on the ground. Characteristically, Your are resolute and persistent, possessing a seemingly untiring endurance that enables you to keep on going through rain or shine. A rural, rather than urban setting appeals to you.

You laugh in the face of danger and succeeds under pressure. You are impulsive and unpredictable at times but will get any task done with efficiency and enthusiasm although you may want to do it alone to make sure it is done right. You like to be obeyed and not the other way around. Even though you can be negative and selfish at times, underneath your thick fur you are sensitive and emotional.

Be careful about your diet, and think of getting more fresh air; indeed, you’ll tend to live too much indoors, forgetting to walk or to maintain contact with nature. There’s a whole world out there for you to explore.

You are sure of yourself and assert your originality, especially in your career, where your qualities are to be acknowledged by your superiors and colleagues. You also have the tendency to adopt overly radical positions at times; try to be more moderate.

You are the enigmatic seducer, charming your senses by their irresistible beauty and inborn wisdom. Graceful and soft-spoken with excellent manners, most people are hopelessly in love with you.

You were career-minded at an early age always vivacious and energetic in your hard work. You have a talent in handling money. Although a true non-conformist and rebellious, you often are a part of the “in-crowd” and dresses fashionably for your friends.

You are understanding about other’s faults and has a compassionate heart, and an introvert by nature, withdrawn and sometimes insecure feeling the need to be loved and protected. The Sheep is the most indecisive of all people and are over-anxious worriers.

Clever, charming, and quick-witted, you are full of tricks up the sleeve to deceive you. Very intelligent and innovative, you are a great problem solver and thrives on challenges. Usually becoming a master at what he chooses to do in life, you are humorous and wants to be the center of attention.

Self-assurance and pride describe you. Always proud of your fine feathers and great lineage, never a day goes by that you do not walk with utmost dignity in the center of attention.

Always the honest and faithful one, you will be with your people through rain or shine and be there when they need you the most. You have a deep sense of loyalty and a passion for justice and you will usually be the first to speak out against any wrongdoing done by any person.

You live to sacrifice yourself to help others and it’s naturally what makes you a stronger person. You may appear as naive and easy to fool but you are really just an optimistic person always believing in the best of people. You don’t say much but you are highly intellectual having a great thirst for knowledge.
